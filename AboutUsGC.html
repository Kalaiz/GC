What's this website for?

Confused on where to go for your Holidays? Well you're in luck, this website will serve as an aid to your bewildered mind on deciding where to go. This is a website which hopes to be a great use for globetrotter.

Why give so much importance to Travelling?

Exploring the world, beyond your country, will always be an enjoyable and memorable one. What's life without any traveling . Wouldn't it be dry and boring without some adventurous moments with your loved ones or friends? Man, since his existence has been exploring and traveling around the world. It has been something which has been done throughout the era and there will be done in years to come.According to a recent survey by Realty Mogul, 47 percent of young people between the ages of 18 and 34 would rather spend their money on traveling than buying a house. This proves that traveling is a trend among the millennial. With advancement in technology traveling has become more convenient and deciding where to go has become even more convenient. 

Why a website based on giving suggestion on where to travel?

Let it be the quench for adventure or to experience different cultures, or just to relax. Travelers have different options of destination to choose from. There are very few websites on which is specifically there for giving suggestion based on the user's interest. Let this website be a helping hand to those travellers who need suggestion on where to travel to .
